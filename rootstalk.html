The Problem

Advisory without infrastructure reaches no one.

Agri organisations — companies, departments, NGOs — invest heavily in agronomic expertise. They know what farmers should do. But the path from that knowledge to a farmer's field has always been broken. Too many gaps. Too many hands. No system connecting them.

Knowledge that doesn't travel

Experts know what farmers should do. But getting the right advice to the right farmer, at the right crop stage, in the right language — that has no system.

Orders that get lost in the chain

A farmer acts on advice but has no reliable path to the correct input, from the right dealer, at the right time. The advisory and the supply chain never meet.

Expertise that can't scale

Agricultural experts exist in abundance. But connecting a farmer's specific problem to the right expert — and routing the answer back — has no infrastructure.

Organisations without visibility

Companies and departments invest in advisory but never know if it reached the farmer. Or worked. There is no feedback loop, no accountability, no proof of impact.

The Ecosystem

Four roles. One connected platform.

RootsTalk is built around the four people who matter in the crop advisory chain. Each has their own view, their own responsibilities, and their own tools — all speaking to each other within the same system.

PWA · Mobile

Farmer

Receives personalised advisory based on their crop, season, and farm conditions. Orders recommended inputs directly. Raises queries when something goes wrong.

Advisory in their language. Brand details revealed only after order approval. Crop history stored and exportable as a QR code.

PWA · Mobile

Dealer

Receives and processes farmer orders. Selects brands, enters volumes and prices, packs and delivers. The supply chain partner who turns advisory into action.

Sees practice type and quantity — never the company's brand recommendations until the order is confirmed. Volume calculated automatically.

PWA · Mobile

Facilitator

The field intermediary. Helps farmers who need support placing orders, routes orders to dealers, and ensures delivery reaches the last mile.

Acts as a trusted bridge for farmers who are less digitally fluent. Carries no order content visibility — only routing and delivery coordination.

PWA · Mobile

FarmPundit

Agricultural experts who answer farmer queries. Onboarded by organisations, routed queries by round-robin, respond within 7 days. Can forward to specialists.

Profile carries education, experience, expertise domains, and crop groups. Primary and Panel Expert types. In-app calling with farmers.

How It Works

From advisory to field — in one system.

RootsTalk turns your organisation's agronomic expertise into a complete operational loop. Your Subject Experts build the advisory once. Every farmer in your network receives it personalised to their conditions. Every order flows back to the right dealer. Every question finds the right expert.



For your organisation, this means one Client Portal — where your team manages advisory, experts, subscriptions, and analytics. For your farmers, this means one app — in their language, built around their crop.

Why RootsTalk

What makes this different from everything else.

01

Personalised, not broadcast

Most agri advisory platforms push the same content to all farmers. RootsTalk delivers a different Package of Practices to each farmer based on their exact crop, season, duration, and irrigation type. The advisory is theirs.

02

Advisory that leads to a delivered input

Most advisory tools stop at the recommendation. RootsTalk closes the loop — from the advisory notification, through the order, through the dealer, to delivery confirmation. One system, end to end.

03

Every farmer's language, not English

The farmer who grows India's food often does not read English. RootsTalk works entirely in the farmer's chosen language — advisory, instructions, notifications, and the app itself. 13 languages, no trade-offs.

04

Brand integrity at every step

Organisations invest in specific products for specific reasons. RootsTalk's locked brand system ensures recommended brands reach farmers through authorised dealers — not substituted, not bypassed.

05

Knowledge from Cosh, not scraped data

RootsTalk's advisory and diagnosis engine runs on Cosh — 50,000+ human-curated knowledge nodes built by Neytiri's agronomy team. Not approximated by AI, not scraped from the internet. Verified agricultural knowledge.

06

Built exclusively for Indian agriculture

Not adapted from a global product. Designed from the first line of code for Indian crops, Indian languages, Indian supply chains, and Indian farmers. The last mile here is different — and we built for it.
